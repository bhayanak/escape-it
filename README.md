# Escape It

A bouncing-ball arcade platformer. Guide your ball upward through 150 levels of spikes, blades, crushers, and moving platforms to reach the exit portal.

## How to Play

- **Desktop**: Use `←` `→` arrow keys to steer the ball left/right
- **Mobile**: Tap the left/right side of the screen to move

The ball bounces automatically. Time your movements to land on platforms, dodge hazards, and collect all 3 ⭐ stars per level for maximum points.

## ⭐ Scoring

| Stars | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| ⭐ | Complete the level |
| ⭐⭐ | Collect all 3 star pickups |
| ⭐⭐⭐ | Collect all stars AND finish under the time target |
